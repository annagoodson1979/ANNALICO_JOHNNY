Our Story

Seal and Stamp Notary was founded with a simple mission: to provide reliable, professional notary services to our community. As a family-owned business, we understand the importance of trust and personal connection.

We specialize in General Notary work, Remote Online Notarization (RON), and In-Person Electronic Notarization (IPEN), offering modern solutions while maintaining the personal touch that only a family business can provide.
